FAQs

What is CAM reconciliation and why does it matter?

It’s the year‑end true‑up between estimated monthly escrow and actual recoverable expenses, per each lease’s rules (caps, stops, gross‑ups, etc.). You’ll send tenants an itemized statement with over/under amounts and support.

Do you support NNN leases?

Yes. We verify pass‑throughs for taxes/insurance and CAM, apply CPI or fixed bumps, and handle partial‑year proration.

How do you handle non‑recoverables?

We map general ledger accounts to recoverable/non‑recoverable buckets based on each lease and create footnotes for any judgment calls.

Is my data secure?

We use encrypted transfer and least‑privilege access. NDAs are available on request.
